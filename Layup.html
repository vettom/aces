Wheelchair Basketball Training

Layup

Chair and ball control with Layup. Pass will be mixed type, challenging player to make quicker shot.

  • Player take 90 Degree turn towards basker before or after designated mark
  • Ball passed to plays when player nearing key. Mix pass type chest/top/bounce
  • Player collect ball and based on position make necessary adjustment and take shot.
  • Similar to above, this time takes ball and stops chair in key before making shot

    • Layup shot from behind screen

  • Player 1 moves down and take swift turn to position behind screen
  • Received ball from top (Mixed pass)
  • Player in front acts as defender
  • Player maked shot
  • Player 2 collects ball and pass to top
