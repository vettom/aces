Wheelchair Basketball Training

Screen and roll

Advantage is less space involved and player 2 has option to score from behind screen forcing action from defender

  • Player 1 positions near key leaving space for bigger defender to jump
  • Player 2 positions near 3 point line
  • Defender jumps to block Player 2
  • Player 1 moves to pick out defender
  • Player 1 or goes for basket depending on which player other defender pick
