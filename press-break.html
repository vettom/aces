Wheelchair Basketball Training

Press-Break

Stage-1 Inbounding

Positioning

  • Few meters from Base line, but with in 1 Bounce throwing reach of Inbounder
  • 2-3 meters from sideline at an angle to Inbounder
  • Position to receive ball in opposit direction to the bloceker
  • When receive ball and turn, make contact with blocker to prevent them reaching ball side
  • One of the receiver must be faster player

Receiving Ball

  • Turn opposit direction to Blocker and move forward at suitable angle
  • Receive ball with one hand away from the blocker
  • Turn the chair and use momentom to throw ball further/faster
  • If not able to pass the ball, move towards second receiver to make 2 man game
  • And/Or away from Inbounder so he/she can move ahead/pick for you
  • Players at half line perform 2 man drill and make themself available to receive ball

Inbound Throw

  • Position at one side of Backet with clear throing angle to both receivers
  • Pass to side and ahead of moving receiver
  • Avoid chest pass as it engages both hands for receiver and they loose momentom
  • No look pass or Fake pass can go long way in getting out of situation.
